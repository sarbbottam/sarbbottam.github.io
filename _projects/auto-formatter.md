Format as you type.

* phone number ```(XXX) XXX-XXXX, XXXXX-XXXXX```
* credit card number ```XXXX XXXX XXXX XXXX, XXXX XXXXXX XXXXX```
* date ```DD/MM/YYYY, MM/DD/YYYY, YYYY/MM/DD```
